Pesto Pasta with Chicken

Description

This pesto pasta with chicken is a great, tasty idea for a dish that can be served for lunch. Be careful though, as the whole meal itself tend to be high in calories. It is due to the fact that it's rich in fats, but these are mostly healthy fats. Therefore, I recommend choosing the portion size wisely. The below recipe and ingredients used in it is a perfect serving for two people. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 bigger skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces (around 300g)
  • 1 pinch of each: salt, pepper, red pepper powder, garlic powder
  • 1 whole cup of pesto sauce (around 190g)
  • ⅓ cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes with herbs, cut into strips

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and place it into a bowl. Add 1 teaspoon of olive oil, all the spices (salt, pepper, red pepper powder, garlic powder) and mix it well.
  2. Fill a large pot with water, add a pinch of salt to it a bring it to boil. Add pasta to the boiling water and after a while reduce the heat by a little. Follow the instructions that shall be provided on the packing of pasta, for it to be prepared al-dente. In most cases this is 8-10 minutes. Drain the pasta after.
  3. In the meantime, heat a little bit of olive oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic until tender, then stir in the chicken mix that we prepared before. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  4. Add pesto into the pan and mix for a while. Then add the sun-dried tomatoes and mix it for short period of time as well. Finally, follow through by adding the pasta and heat it over low to medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes, mixing it at the same time. Make sure that pesto covers your pasta evenly!

  5. OPTIONAL

    While performing the previous step, you could also try adding some pasta water into the pan. It is used to make the pesto creamier!
