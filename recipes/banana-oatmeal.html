Banana Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a great, healthy dish, full of carbohydrates that provides you with a large dose of energy. That is why it is widely used as a breakfast meal. This recipe makes it even tastier, thanks to the additional ingredients that we will use. If you like both bananas & oatmeal, then you should definitely try the recipe yourself. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup of oat flakes
  • ¾ cup of milk
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of date flakes
  • 1 teaspoon of liquid honey

Steps

  1. Add the oat flakes and milk into a pot and heat it over a medium heat, stirring it slowly all the time. Stir it until you notice that the oat flakes absorbed enough milk, and the milk became more creamy than liquid.
  2. In the meantime, cut the banana into slices and then mash them in a bowl that you'll serve the Oatmeal in.
  3. Once you see that the oat flakes & milk are ready, transfer them from the pot to the bowl with mashed bananas. Add chia seeds, date flakes and liquid honey.
  4. Mix it well and let it rest for 1 to 2 minutes before eating.
